Who are we?

Stone & Hunger is a non-profit public charitable organization established with the idea of promoting peace, inculcating socio-religious tolerance among the people to foster unity and brotherhood and lastly, to help the poor, especially in the fields of education and healthcare.

Learn more
Our Objectives
  • To promote peace and social harmony among the masses and instill the feeling of universal brotherhood.
  • Offer scholarships to needy and deserving students.
  • Establish educational institutions to serve masses
  • Conduct social service programmes for society's betterment like distribution of food packets, blankets and clothes to homeless.
  • Respond to the needs of orphans by providing them career assistance and social guidance.
  • To equip youths and fresh graduates with soft skills that help them find the job of their choice.
  • To help single-mothers and widows become self-reliant.
  • Organise meetings, lectures, discussions, seminars, symposiums, competitions and debates on social and environmental issues.

Our Vision

To make a difference in the lives of people by promoting peace and charity

General Aid

It is one of the aids granted by Stone & Hunger, wherein we largely provide financial support or loans for the purposes of rent, daily expenditure and medicines to the underprivileged and down-trodden community. The financial support is either given by the organization or through the funds generated by our generous donors.

Educational Aid

Education and knowledge are one of the forefront values of Stone & Hunger. Thus, the organization partakes immensely in providing financial aid for those deserving students who are unable to pay for their tuition fees. The financial aid is either given by the organization or through the funds generated by our generous donors.

Medicine Aid

Apart from financial aids, Stone & Hunger assists the members of the underprivileged community who do not have proper access to healthcare, by providing monthly medication for those suffering with diabetes, hypertension and other long-standing illnesses.

Provisional Aid

Stone & Hunger has taken part in providing provisional aids by arranging fixed monthly ration to financially poor widows, orphans and families. We also conduct yearly food and ration kit distributions among the needy people.

Achievements So Far

  • Stone & Hunger has provided educational aid in the form of scholarships to the tune of ₹Z9.5 lakh to poor, needy and meritorious students which include those pursuing BDS and MBBS degrees.
  • Stone & Hunger has spent over ₹13 lakh towards medical help for complex treatment procedures and monthly medicines.
  • Assistance to poor and shelterless by way of providing free blankets during winter.
  • Frequent visits to orphanages to connect with deprived children emotionally and motivating them to learn, achieve and grow.
  • Arranged livelihood source by purchasing pushcart and investing in buying an auto to make individuals self-reliant.
  • Montly-aid to widows and deserted senior citizens.
  • Helping those struck by natural calamities by organising campaigns to collect basic supplies like clothes, food packets and medicines. Our team was engaged in relief activities during floods in Karnataka, Kerala and West Bengal.
