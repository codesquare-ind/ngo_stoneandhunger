About the NGO

The Information for Guidance and Education Trust (iGET) is a non-profit public charitable organization established with the idea of promoting peace, inculcating socio-religious tolerance among the people to foster unity and brotherhood and lastly, to help the poor, especially in the fields of education and healthcare.
Our team works relentlessly in identifying the deserving people who require financial help to attain a sustainable livelihood. We also do a complete screening of our beneficiaries so that the efforts of our benefactors do not end up in the hands of the undeserving.
We are financed solely through fundraising and receive no government or institutional funding.

Our Vision

To make a difference in the lives of people by promoting peace and charity
Our Aims & Objectives
  1. To promote peace and social harmony among the masses and instill the feeling of universal brotherhood.
  2. Offer scholarships to needy and deserving students.
  3. Establish educational institutions to serve masses
  4. Conduct social service programmes for society's betterment like distribution of food packets, blankets and clothes to homeless.
  5. Respond to the needs of orphans by providing them career assistance and social guidance.
  6. To equip youths and fresh graduates with soft skills that help them find the job of their choice.
  7. To help single-mothers and widows become self-reliant.
  8. Organise meetings, lectures, discussions, seminars, symposiums, competitions and debates on social and environmental issues.
